
# MechRoute

MechRoute is a vehicle repair assistance and service booking platform designed to address real world challenges faced by vehicle users during breakdowns and routine servicing.

## Problem Statement

Vehicle users often face two major challenges in their daily lives.

Firstly, unexpected vehicle breakdowns such as tyre punctures, battery failures, or engine issues can occur at any time. In such situations, especially in unfamiliar locations, it becomes difficult to quickly find a reliable nearby mechanic. This leads to delays, safety concerns, and inconvenience.

Secondly, many vehicle owners struggle to schedule regular vehicle servicing due to busy daily routines and time constraints. Existing systems do not provide a unified solution for both emergency assistance and planned servicing.

Therefore, there is a need for a platform that efficiently handles both sudden breakdown situations and routine service scheduling in a simple and accessible manner.

## Proposed Solution

MechRoute provides a location based platform that connects vehicle users with nearby mechanics. The system supports both on spot roadside assistance and pre booked vehicle servicing. This ensures quick response during emergencies and convenient scheduling for maintenance.
